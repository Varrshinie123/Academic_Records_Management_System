Botox – Muscle Relaxing Injections

BONTA – Botulinum toxin A

Botox – Muscle Relaxing Injections

This is a protein that is injected into wrinkled skin; it works by temporarily weakening the muscles responsible for the wrinkles.

When administered by an expert it is a safe and effective treatment of wrinkles that are made prominent by facial expression. It has to be re-injected every 4 – 6 months to maintain the effect.
